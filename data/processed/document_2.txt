# Personal Loan Credit Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs underwriting decisions for unsecured personal loans.

---

## 2. Applicant Eligibility

Applicants must:

* Be at least 21 years old.
* Have stable employment history of at least 12 months.
* Maintain an active bank account.
* Meet minimum income requirements.

---

## 3. Credit Score Evaluation

| Credit Score | Lending Decision |
| ------------ | ---------------- |
| Above 760    | Excellent        |
| 700 - 759    | Good             |
| 650 - 699    | Acceptable       |
| Below 650    | High Risk        |

Applications below 650 generally require rejection.

---

## 4. Income Requirements

Minimum annual income:

* Salaried applicants: ₹500,000
* Self-employed applicants: ₹750,000

---

## 5. Employment Stability

Preferred employment tenure:

* Minimum 12 months with current employer.
* Minimum 24 months business operation for self-employed applicants.

---

## 6. Loan-to-Income Assessment

Recommended loan amount:

* Not more than 10 times monthly income.

Applications exceeding this threshold require enhanced review.

---

## 7. Risk Indicators

The following increase risk:

* Multiple recent credit inquiries.
* Significant recent increase in debt.
* High revolving credit utilization.
* Irregular repayment history.

---

## 8. Decline Conditions

Applications should be declined when:

* Income verification fails.
* Fraud indicators are confirmed.
* Active bankruptcy exists.
* Credit score is below policy threshold.
