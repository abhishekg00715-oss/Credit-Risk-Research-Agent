# Premium Credit Card Eligibility Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines eligibility requirements for premium and rewards-based credit card products.

---

## 2. Target Customer Segment

Premium credit cards are intended for:

* High-income professionals.
* Business owners.
* Affluent retail customers.
* Existing customers with strong repayment history.

---

## 3. Minimum Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must satisfy all of the following:

* Age between 25 and 65 years.
* Credit score of at least 750.
* Annual income of at least ₹1,500,000.
* No delinquency within the previous 24 months.

---

## 4. Preferred Customer Characteristics

Preference may be given to customers who:

* Maintain utilization below 30%.
* Hold existing banking relationships.
* Demonstrate stable employment.
* Maintain low debt obligations.

---

## 5. Credit Limit Assignment

| Credit Score | Indicative Credit Limit |
| ------------ | ----------------------- |
| 750 - 799    | ₹300,000 - ₹500,000     |
| 800 - 849    | ₹500,000 - ₹1,000,000   |
| 850+         | Above ₹1,000,000        |

Final limits remain subject to underwriting review.

---

## 6. Review Triggers

Applications require manual review when:

* Utilization exceeds 50%.
* Income documentation is incomplete.
* DTI exceeds 40%.
* Credit history is less than 24 months.

---

## 7. Automatic Rejection Criteria

Applications must be rejected when:

* Credit score is below 750.
* Fraud alerts are present.
* Bankruptcy is reported.
* Material misrepresentation is identified.

---

## 8. Approval Principles

Premium card approvals should prioritize:

* Strong repayment history.
* Sustainable income.
* Low leverage.
* Long-term customer value.
