**Credit Risk Research Agent**

 **Vision** 

To build an AI-powered Credit Risk Research Assistant that helps credit analysts, risk managers, and product teams quickly research policies, evaluate customer risk, analyze portfolio trends, and generate explainable recommendations.

**Problem Statement**

Credit risk assessment often requires navigating multiple sources of information:

Lending policies
Underwriting guidelines
Customer credit profiles
Historical portfolio performance
Regulatory requirements

Analysts spend significant time gathering and synthesizing information before making a recommendation.

**Proposed Solution**

A multi-agent AI platform that:

Retrieves information from credit policy documents
Analyzes customer profiles
Evaluates portfolio-level risk trends
Produces explainable recommendations
Provides source-backed reasoning
