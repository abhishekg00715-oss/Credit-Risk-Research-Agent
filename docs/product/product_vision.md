# Product Vision

## Vision

To build a modular, explainable, AI-powered Credit Decision Intelligence Platform that enables financial institutions to make faster, more consistent, and evidence-based lending decisions through collaborative AI agents specializing in policy interpretation, customer assessment, portfolio intelligence, recommendations, and governance.

---

# Problem Statement

Credit decisioning is a complex process that requires analysts to gather, interpret, and reconcile information from multiple independent sources before arriving at a well-supported recommendation.

These sources typically include:

- Lending and underwriting policies
- Customer credit and financial profiles
- Portfolio risk and performance metrics
- Regulatory and compliance requirements
- Market and economic intelligence
- Historical lending decisions and supporting evidence

Analysts often spend considerable time locating relevant information, validating policy compliance, performing manual risk analysis, and documenting the rationale behind their recommendations. This process can be time-consuming, inconsistent, difficult to audit, and challenging to scale.

---

# Proposed Solution

Develop a local-first, framework-independent, multi-agent AI platform where specialized agents collaborate to provide comprehensive and explainable credit decision support.

The platform will progressively deliver capabilities to:

- Interpret lending policies using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Assess customer creditworthiness using deterministic business rules
- Analyze portfolio risk, trends, concentrations, and benchmarks
- Generate evidence-based lending recommendations
- Explain decisions with supporting evidence and policy references
- Orchestrate collaboration between multiple specialized AI agents
- Support future capabilities including fraud assessment, regulatory intelligence, market intelligence, collections analytics, and customer opportunity identification

The platform is designed around modular services, reusable components, explainable AI principles, and enterprise-grade architectural practices, allowing new capabilities and agents to be added incrementally without disrupting existing functionality.

---

# Target Users

The platform is intended to support:

- Credit Analysts
- Credit Risk Managers
- Underwriters
- Portfolio Managers
- Risk & Compliance Teams
- Product Managers
- Business Analysts
- Data Scientists

---

# Key Product Objectives

- Reduce the time required to perform customer and portfolio risk assessments.
- Improve consistency and transparency of credit decisions.
- Provide explainable recommendations supported by policy evidence and business rules.
- Demonstrate scalable multi-agent orchestration using a modular architecture.
- Enable future expansion into enterprise-wide credit and banking intelligence capabilities.

# Success Metrics

| Metric | Description | Target (Initial) |
|---------|-------------|------------------|
| Policy Retrieval Accuracy | Accuracy of retrieving relevant policy content for user queries | ≥ 90% |
| Intent Routing Accuracy | Correct routing of user requests to the appropriate agent(s) | ≥ 95% |
| Customer Assessment Accuracy | Correct application of deterministic business rules | 100% |
| Recommendation Explainability | Recommendations include supporting rationale and evidence | 100% |
| Source Attribution Coverage | Policy-based responses include citations | 100% |
| Response Time | End-to-end response for common scenarios | < 3 seconds (excluding first-time model initialization) |
| Multi-Agent Workflow Success | Successful orchestration of all required agents | ≥ 95% |
| Extensibility | Ability to integrate new agents with minimal changes to existing components | Qualitative |
| User Experience | Analyst satisfaction with usability and response presentation | To be evaluated |
